Our Story

At Warung Cik Siti, we bring the authentic flavors of Malaysian street food to your table. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, maintaining the traditional taste while incorporating fresh, local ingredients.

Since opening our doors in 2020, we've been dedicated to sharing our family's culinary heritage with our community. Every dish is prepared with care, bringing you the true taste of Malaysian hawker-style cooking.
